The real story

Meet
Sunday Rax

“Around here, we do not just survive. We SOAR.”

Sunday Rax is a woman who knows what it means to build while becoming.

Before the books, brands, ministry, strategy, and businesses, Sunday knew what it felt like to live stretched thin—working a 9–5, barely making it from check to check, and still carrying dreams that were bigger than her circumstances. There were days she went to work with no lunch money and no food to bring from home, but still showed up with purpose pulling on her.

Her breakthrough did not come from a perfect plan. It came from obedience. In a spur-of-the-moment leap of faith, Sunday quit her job and pursued her own tax business. In her very first tax season, she made what she had previously earned in an entire year—in just four months.

That moment did not just change her income. It changed her understanding of what was possible when faith, strategy, discipline, and obedience came together.

Today, Sunday is an ordained pastor, prophetess, author, entrepreneur, financial strategist, tax professional, speaker, mentor, podcast host, wife, and mother. She is the founder of Rax Financial & Tax Solutions, LLC, the Founder and Lead Pastor of SOAR Global Ministries, owner of LAY’D Luxury Co., visionary behind Reign Water, founder of Drivers on Deck Recruiting, and Executive Director of The Daily Bread Project.

But Sunday does not carry those titles for show. Every lane of her life is connected to one assignment: helping people heal, build, obey God, and move.

He healed.He restored.He redirected.He rebuilt.

Through ministry, books, business, financial strategy, mentorship, and real-life teaching, Sunday helps people stop pretending, get clear, fix what needs to be fixed, and rise from the place life tried to leave them.

Her style is bold, warm, funny, honest, and full of faith. She will pray with you, laugh with you, tell you the truth, help you fix the paperwork, and remind you that you are not done yet.

What looked like survival became preparation.
